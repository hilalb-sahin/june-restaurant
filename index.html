About Us

June Kyoto is, quite simply, unique.

Unique because it truly puts the authenticity & quality of its food 1st.

June Kyoto makes its food fresh in small batches throughout the day or to order using genuine Japanese ingredients, such as sushi rice and nori (toasted seaweed), for making its sushi.

Our fish supplier only works in partnership with fisheries that are MSC (Marine Stewardship Council) certified to ensure the fish we use is from sustainable fishing practices and we only use fresh British chicken and pork for our hot food menu.
